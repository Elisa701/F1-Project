Las Vegas Circuit

Technical Details

Location: Las Vegas, USA

Length: 5.281 km

Laps: 58

First GP: 2019

Curiosities

The Las Vegas Strip Circuit hosts the Las Vegas Grand Prix and is one of the most spectacular and unique venues in Formula 1. It is famous for its high-speed layout along the iconic Las Vegas Strip and its dazzling night-race atmosphere. Since its debut, the circuit has quickly become a major highlight of the championship, combining world-class racing with the city's renowned entertainment scene. The track was specifically designed to showcase the skyline of Las Vegas while providing multiple overtaking opportunities and exciting wheel-to-wheel action.
