Circuit Zandvoort

Technical Details

Location: Zandvoort, Netherlands

Length: 4.259 km

Laps: 72

First GP: 1952

Curiosities

Zandvoort is a historic coastal circuit that returned to Formula 1 in 2021 after decades away. Famous for its banked corners and sandy surroundings, it offers a unique old-school racing feel. The passionate Dutch crowd creates an intense atmosphere, especially supporting Max Verstappen. Its layout rewards precision and bravery.
