Silverstone Circuit

Technical Details

Location: Silverstone, United Kingdom

Length: 5.891 km

Laps: 52

First GP: 1950

Curiosities

Silverstone is considered the birthplace of Formula 1, hosting the first World Championship race in 1950. Originally a World War II airfield, it has evolved into a fast, flowing high-speed circuit. Famous corners like Copse, Maggots, and Becketts test aerodynamics and bravery. It remains the spiritual home of British motorsport.
