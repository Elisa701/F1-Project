Circuit of the Americas

Technical Details

Location: Austin, USA

Length: 5.513 km

Laps: 56

First GP: 2012

Curiosities

The Circuit of the Americas (COTA) is the main United States Grand Prix venue and was designed specifically for Formula 1. It debuted in 2012 and quickly became one of the most popular modern circuits. Inspired by several classic tracks, it features elevation changes and a mix of technical and high-speed sections. It has become central to F1’s growth in the American market.
