Monaco Circuit

Technical Details

Location: Monte Carlo, Monaco

Length: 3.337 km

Laps: 78

First GP: 1950

Curiosities

Monaco is the most prestigious and historic circuit in Formula 1, first raced in 1929 and part of the championship since 1950. Its narrow streets, tight corners, and elevation changes make it one of the most challenging circuits in the world. Overtaking is extremely difficult, making qualifying performance crucial. Winning here is considered one of the greatest achievements in motorsport.
