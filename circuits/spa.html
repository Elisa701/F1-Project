Circuit de Spa‑Francorchamps

Technical Details

Location: Stavelot, Belgium

Lenght: 7.004 km

Laps: 44

First GP: 1950

Curiosities

Spa-Francorchamps is one of the most legendary circuits in motorsport, located in the Ardennes forest. Known for unpredictable weather and dramatic elevation changes, it has been part of F1 since the championship’s early years. Iconic corners like Eau Rouge and Raidillon are globally famous. The circuit is often a favorite among drivers for its raw, natural layout.
