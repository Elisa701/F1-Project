Audi

Drivers: Nico Hülkenberg, Gabriel Bortoleto

Team Principal: Mattia Binotto

Base: Hinwil, Switzerland

Power Unit: Audi

History: Audi’s Formula 1 project represents one of the most ambitious manufacturer entries in modern motorsport, built around a long-term plan to become a championship-winning works team. The German brand officially committed to F1 for the 2026 regulation era, focusing on hybrid power unit innovation, sustainability, and technological leadership. The project is based on the historic Sauber organization in Hinwil, which has competed in Formula 1 since 1993 under various identities, including BMW Sauber during its factory partnership era. Sauber is known for its strong engineering culture and its role in developing future stars, while maintaining a reputation as a resilient independent team. With Audi’s full factory backing, significant investment, and long-term factory infrastructure expansion, the team entered a completely new era in 2026. The goal is to transform Sauber’s solid foundations into a future front-running operation capable of fighting for podiums and world championships within the new regulatory cycle.
