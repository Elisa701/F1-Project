Cadillac

Drivers: Sergio Pérez, Valtteri Bottas

Team Principal: Graeme Lowdon

Base: Indianapolis, USA

Power Unit: Ferrari

History: Cadillac Formula 1 Team represents one of the most ambitious new projects in modern Formula 1. Backed by General Motors and developed in partnership with TWG Motorsports, the team was officially approved to join the Formula 1 grid in 2026. The entry marks Cadillac’s first direct involvement as a Formula 1 constructor and reflects the growing interest of American manufacturers in the championship. Building its operations across the United States and Europe, the team aims to establish a long-term presence in the sport by combining advanced engineering, significant investment, and experienced personnel recruited from across the motorsport industry. While immediate success is unlikely, Cadillac enters Formula 1 with the objective of developing into a competitive force over the coming years and strengthening its global performance brand.
