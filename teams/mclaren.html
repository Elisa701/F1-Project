McLaren

Drivers: Oscar Piastri, Lando Norris

Team Principal: Andrea Stella

Base: Woking, United Kingdom

Power Unit: Mercedes

History: McLaren is one of Formula 1’s most historic and celebrated teams, having competed in the sport since 1966 after being founded by New Zealand driver Bruce McLaren. Over the decades, McLaren established itself as one of the defining names in motorsport history through partnerships with legendary drivers such as Ayrton Senna, Alain Prost, Niki Lauda, Mika Häkkinen, Emerson Fittipaldi, and Lewis Hamilton. The team’s fierce rivalry with Ferrari and dominance during the late 1980s remain among the most iconic chapters in Formula 1 history. Following difficult periods during the Honda reunion years and the early hybrid era, McLaren underwent a major restructuring process focused on infrastructure, technical leadership, and long-term stability. Under CEO Zak Brown and Team Principal Andrea Stella, the team gradually returned to competitiveness, eventually re-emerging as a championship contender. McLaren’s 2025 title-winning season with Lando Norris marked the culmination of years of rebuilding and firmly re-established the team among Formula 1’s elite. By 2026, McLaren was widely viewed as one of the strongest and most complete organizations on the grid.
