Alexander Albon

Team: Williams Racing

Team Principal: James Vowles

Number: 23

Nationality: Thailand

Debut: 2019

Born: 23 March 1996

History: Thai-British driver Alexander Albon successfully rebuilt his Formula 1 career after a challenging spell at Red Bull Racing. Returning with Williams, Albon impressed the paddock through his ability to consistently outperform the car’s expected potential, particularly in race conditions. Known for calm communication, tire preservation, and adaptability, he became a central figure in Williams’ resurgence. By 2026, Albon was widely appreciated for his maturity, resilience, and leadership within the team.
