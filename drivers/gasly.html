Pierre Gasly

Team: Alpine F1 Team

Team Principal: Flavio Briatore

Number: 10

Nationality: France

Debut: 2017

Born: 7 February 1996

History: French driver Pierre Gasly established himself as one of Formula 1’s most resilient competitors after overcoming setbacks earlier in his career. His victory at the 2020 Italian Grand Prix became one of the sport’s most celebrated underdog stories and highlighted his speed and determination. Driving for Alpine, Gasly combined strong qualifying performances with aggressive racecraft and technical consistency. By 2026, he remained an essential part of Alpine’s efforts to challenge the leading teams more consistently.
