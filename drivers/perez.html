Sergio Pérez

Team: Cadillac F1 Team

Team Principal: Gremae Lowdon

Number: 11

Nationality: Mexico

Debut: 2011

Born: 26 January 1990

History: Mexican driver Sergio Pérez built his Formula 1 reputation through exceptional tire management, strategic awareness, and consistency across long race stints. After achieving major success with Red Bull Racing and contributing to multiple Constructors’ Championships, Pérez brought his experience and racecraft to Cadillac’s Formula 1 project. Known for his calm approach and ability to deliver strong performances in difficult situations, he remained one of the grid’s most experienced and dependable drivers entering 2026.
