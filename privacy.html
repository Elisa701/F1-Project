Privacy Policy

This page explains how user data is handled within the “F1 Fan Project” website.

1. Purpose of the Website

This website is a university project dedicated to the 2026 Formula 1 World Championship. It does not collect personal data for commercial purposes and does not use advanced tracking systems.

2. Data Collected

The website does not require registration and does not collect personally identifiable information. However, anonymous technical data may be collected automatically by the browser, such as:

  • masked IP address
  • device and browser type
  • visited pages
  • time spent on the website

This information is not used for profiling.

3. Cookies

The website uses only technical cookies necessary for proper functionality. No profiling cookies or third‑party cookies are used.

4. Contact Form

If the user submits a message through a contact form, the provided data (name, email, message) is used solely to respond to the request. No data is stored or shared.

5. External Links

The website may contain links to external resources (e.g., Wikipedia, official F1 websites). We are not responsible for their privacy policies.
